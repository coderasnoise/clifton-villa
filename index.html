Our Story

Friendly, welcoming, and highly recommended as offering an exceptional Southport guesthouse experience, Clifton Villa Hotel sits in the heart of beautiful Southport, nestled between historic Lord Street and the Promenade’s Marine Lake waterfront, making it the ideal hotel for any short break.

Step inside this beautiful Southport hotel and you’ll be greeted warmly by Atty and Aynur Korkut, whose passion for great hospitality has been welcoming guests to their hotel. This warmth of hospitality, evident since 1983, is recognized by the generous guest feedback that has earned Clifton Villa Hotel the rare induction to the TripAdvisor Hall of Fame for Service Excellence and with Certificate of Excellence awarded four time as well.

Contemporary Southport Guesthouse | Timeless Southport Hospitality

A quality Southport hotel maintained to the highest standards, Clifton Villa has been recently redecorated to offer guests the ultimate in modern comfort and convenience.

Consistently rated as one of the best hotels in Southport, Clifton Villa is the perfect place to relax and unwind.

Policies

  1. The hotel was built in or around 1900 and the design and build of the hotel does give some access issues.
  2. The hotel is not wheelchair friendly and has uneven surfaces and steps that may not be suitable for all users.
  3. There are three steps up to the hotel and two steps down to the dining room and the design of the building does not allow space for ramps at most of these points.
  4. Rooms 7 & 8 are located on the 2nd floor and are accessed by a narrower and slightly steeper set of stairs.
  5. The proprietors will always try to accommodate guests as best they can, but recommend that if you will have or even think you will have access issues when you stay with us, that you discuss your individual needs with the hotel staff prior to making your booking, the hotel cannot be held responsible for any inconvenience caused if you do not discuss your needs prior to booking.
  6. During the hours of darkness and especially during periods of inclement weather, extra care is needed at the front of the hotel as there are steps and uneven surfaces that may be obscured or appear hidden by the shadows cast by vehicles parked in the car park. The proprietors can accept no liability for any damage to yourself, your property, vehicles or clothing if the above advice given here is ignored or not followed.

  1. Rooms are normally available for check in from 2pm onwards – this may not always be possible and guests should check on day of travel.
  2. In certain circumstances, it may be possible to check-in earlier - please check on day of travel.
  3. No check-ins are allowed after 8.30pm unless by prior arrangement.
  4. Guests are asked to check out by 10.30am on the day of departure - failure to do this may incur extra charges.
  5. On check-in all guests are required to complete a guest registration form.
  6. Foreign nationals will need to produce identity documents - we are required by law to take a copy of this ID.

  1. All deposits are non-refundable.
  2. Deposits are normally taken on a per person basis at a rate of £30.00 per person.
  3. Should the booked stay be within 7 days of the booking date, we will normally ask for the whole amount of the stay to be paid for up front.
  4. This amount then becomes the deposit and any cancellation thereafter will make the full amount non-refundable.
  5. For bookings not covered by point 3) above, in the event of a no show or a cancellation with less than 2 days notice prior to the date of arrival, an amount equivalent to 100% of the total booking value will be charged to the card used to pay the deposit.
  6. For bookings already paid for, if we receive no contact before 8.30pm or a booker does not turn up at the hotel before 8.30pm on the agreed day of arrival, we will class this as a no-show and any payments already made will be non-refundable.
  7. Accommodation charges are non-refundable once the booked stay has commenced.

  1. We accept payments by cash and credit or debit card.
  2. Customers paying with cash are required to provide identification on check-in.
  3. Acceptable forms of ID are: current driving license, ID card, passport, credit card or switch card, utility bill, bank statement (with address) or police warrant card.

  1. This is a no smoking hotel, extra charges will be payable if you have been smoking in your room.
  2. The proprietors cannot accept responsibility for the loss of guests' property unless handed in for safe custody.
  3. We reserve the right and you hereby authorise us to charge your debit or credit card for any damage incurred to your room or the hotel during your stay (including without limitation specialist cleaning) or for any items that are missing when you leave. For cash payers, a separate bill will be produced.

  1. Mealtimes are prearranged and no allowance can be made for meals not taken.
  2. Alcohol or snacks consumed from the honesty bar or any extras provided are charged separately and are not included in the room rate.

  1. The proprietors reserve the right to amend, curtail or terminate any contract should the conduct of any person who is party to that contract cause any detriment to the hotel or well being of other guests.
  2. No refunds will be given in this event.
  3. The proprietors also reserve the right to refuse entry to any guest who turns up intoxicated beyond comprehension or whose personal hygiene or behaviour is such that other guests or members of staff would complain.
  4. No refunds would be given on these occasions.
